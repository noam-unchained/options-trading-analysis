# Options Trading Analysis

Tools and documentation focused on executing and tracking the Wheel Strategy (selling cash-secured puts and covered calls).

### Core Focus
* **The Wheel Strategy:** Comprehensive tracking of option premium collection, assignments, and underlying stock cost-basis adjustments.
* **Portfolio Tracking:** Custom models designed to fill the gaps left by standard broker platforms, providing better visualization of long-term strategy performance and risk management.

### Tools & Files
* Strategy tracking sheets and risk calculation models.
